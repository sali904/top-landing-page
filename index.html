An ode to the city

In the midst of the hustle and bustle of the city, we are reminded that life is going on even in moments of stillness. Here is a collection of photos that capture the juxtaposition beautifully, encouraging us to pause and embrace the moment - and then to keep going.

There's beauty within the busy intersection of the shopping district...

...and within the four walls of a low-key restaurant.

There's so many places that we have been...

...and so many more places that we have yet to.

“A city isn’t so unlike a person. They both have the marks to show they have many stories to tell. They see many faces. They tear things down and make new again.”

―Rasmenia Massoud, “Broken Abroad”
